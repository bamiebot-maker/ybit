Community overview

Simple, practical, and built for real growth

YBIT brings together Yoruba students, developers, designers, and builders who want to keep learning and keep building. The community is open to both male and female members and creates space for support, discipline, collaboration, and impact.

Mission

Help Yoruba builders learn faster, build better, and grow together in tech.

Vision

Create a strong pipeline of Yoruba tech talent with skill, confidence, and community.

Values

Growth, collaboration, discipline, consistency, inclusion, and useful work.

About YBIT

A welcoming space for Yoruba tech builders

YBIT is for people who want more than learning alone. It is a community for people who want to practice, ask questions, work with others, and keep moving forward with support.

Inclusive community

Open to both male and female tech enthusiasts across software, design, product, data, and other builder paths.

Clear focus

The community stays focused on growth, collaboration, discipline, and impact instead of noise.

Real support

Members learn with others, find accountability, and build momentum with a stronger network around them.
